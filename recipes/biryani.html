Biryani

Description

A fragrant and flavorful one-pot dish, Biryani is a popular South Asian rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, tender meat or vegetables, and a blend of spices. Itâ€™s layered and cooked to perfection with ghee, saffron, and caramelized onions, resulting in a delicious, hearty meal. Often served with cooling raita or salad, Biryani is perfect for festive occasions or a lavish dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1/4 tsp saffron soaked in 2 tbsp milk
  • 4 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Whole spices: 2 bay leaves, 4 cardamoms, 4 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice with bay leaves, cardamom, and salt until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, fry onions until golden. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, and chicken to the pan. Cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Layer the chicken and rice alternately in a pot. Drizzle saffron milk and fried onions on top.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes. Serve hot with raita.
