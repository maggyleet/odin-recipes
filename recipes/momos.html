Momos

Description

Momos are steamed dumplings popular in Tibetan, Nepalese, and Indian cuisines. They consist of a soft flour dough filled with a savory mixture of vegetables or minced meat, seasoned with soy sauce and spices. These bite-sized delights are served with spicy dipping sauces, offering a satisfying, warm, and flavorful snack or appetizer./p>

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water (for dough)
  • 1 cup finely chopped vegetables (cabbage, carrot, onion) or minced chicken
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and water to make a soft dough. Cover and rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Combine vegetables or chicken with soy sauce, ginger, salt, and pepper for filling.
  3. Roll dough into thin circles, place filling in the center, and fold edges to seal.
  4. Steam in a greased steamer for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
